About

The AI Division of VIT Bhopal is organizing an International Three Day Workshop on “Industrial Applications of Artificial Intelligence in Sensing, Automation, Process Control, Production and Quality Assurance towards Industry 4.0 (IAAI)” during April 7-9, 2022. The workshop will gather many International and National delegates and research scholars along with the expert speakers from Industry, Academia and R&D sectors to ponder upon various industries
