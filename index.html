INSIGHT

VIT Bhopal University has a goal with a new global outlook that empowers its aspirants to attain excellence through learning. The objective is to educate students from all over India, and from other countries, for them to become enlightened individuals, improving the standards of the society.

Here at VIT Bhopal’s AI Division, we give credence to practical skills and hands-on experience to develop and shape the career orientation of students.

AI Club is the newly launched technical club of VIT Bhopal. Where we promise to deliver far-ranging knowledge about Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to students


The AI Division of VIT Bhopal is organizing an International Three Day Workshop on “Industrial Applications of Artificial Intelligence in Sensing, Automation, Process Control, Production and Quality Assurance towards Industry 4.0 (IAAI)” during April 7-9, 2022. The workshop will gather many International and National delegates and research scholars along with the expert speakers from Industry, Academia and R&D sectors to ponder upon various industries such as
