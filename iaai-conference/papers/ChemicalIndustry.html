Chemical Industry



Artificial Intelligence is an emerging technology and currently experiencing a renaissance in the development of the latest methods and practical applications for the ongoing methods in the Chemical Industry. AI has the massive potential to reduce energy consumption, various environmental burdens, and operational hazards of chemical production. The conference aims to present a combination of aspects of Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning methods for the applications in Chemistry and Chemical Engineering touching upon the important areas having high probabilities of development and progress.

Topics for submission

  • Unit Operations and Processes
  • Chemical Plants
  • Supply Chain
  • Mineral Exploration
  • Instrumentation: Monitoring and Data Analysis
