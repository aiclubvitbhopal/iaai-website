SMART AND NANOMATERIALS



Smart materials are the type of materials that can be made to respond to external stimuli by modifying their properties accordingly. These materials are a major part of several fields ranging from medicine to self-repairing gadgets and buildings. However, to tap into their full potential, it is imperative to map their precise behavior. This can be achieved by the involvement of Artificial Intelligence such as virtual testing tools. Some of the methods include Bayesian Inverse Modeling and Piecewise-Deterministic Markov Processes. This advanced science can bring about a revolution in the generation of smart models for materials and multiscale simulation frameworks. AI converges with nanotechnology in remarkable ways. Microscopy, Chemical Modelling, Nano-computing, etc are some of the advancements. Machine Learning has improved tasks like Visualization, One-shot learning, Molecular Dynamics, and Nano combinatorics, to a great extent.
